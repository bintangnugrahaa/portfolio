Many local laundry businesses struggle with manual bookkeeping, lost physical receipts, and disorganized customer data. Relying on paper-based methods often leads to untracked operational expenses, unclaimed laundry items, and difficulty in calculating accurate monthly profits. The primary challenge was to build a reliable, accessible digital system that staff could use with minimal training to manage these daily operations efficiently.

To solve this, I developed a native web-based Point of Sale (POS) application tailored specifically for laundry services using PHP and MySQL. The system features a clean, responsive admin dashboard that allows cashiers and administrators to effortlessly manage customer information, track incoming and outgoing laundry, and process payments. I integrated specific modules for tracking unpaid orders, logging daily operational expenses, and generating printable transaction receipts to ensure transparent documentation.

The implementation of this POS system completely digitized the business workflow, eliminating the risks associated with lost physical records. Business owners can now easily monitor daily transactions in real-time, track precise profit and loss metrics through automated monthly reporting, and improve customer service with highly organized order tracking. The platform ensures better accountability and modernizes the entire laundry management process.
