Educational institutions frequently rely on manual attendance tracking, a process that is time-consuming, susceptible to inaccuracies, and lacks transparency for parents. The challenge was to develop an automated system that could not only handle rapid, concurrent attendance logging during morning rush hours but also bridge the communication gap between the school administration and students' families.

To solve this, I engineered a native PHP web application featuring a specialized "Kiosk Mode" designed for seamless interaction with physical QR and RFID scanners. The core innovation lies in its asynchronous notification engine—using a dedicated WhatsApp worker, the system automatically dispatches real-time arrival and departure messages directly to parents' phones the moment a student scans their ID. Additionally, the administrative dashboard was equipped with robust tools, including Excel data import/export capabilities, automated monthly recapitulations, and a feature to generate and print physical student ID cards.

The deployment of this platform drastically modernized the school's daily operations. It eliminated the administrative bottleneck of manual data entry and provided parents with immediate peace of mind regarding their children's whereabouts. The system ensures high data accuracy, strengthens school security, and sets a new standard for parent-school communication.
